    Introduction

    SSHxy is an open-source web-based access management platform for SSH connections. SSHxy builds on the power of SSH, providing management tools and features, such as granular user controls, connection recording and playback, etc., to make managing remote servers and cloud infrastructure over SSH more enterprise friendly.

    SSHxy is maintained by The Binary Workshop , with commercial support available if needed.
